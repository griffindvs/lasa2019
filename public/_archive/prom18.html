Prom 2018

Where: UT Etter-Harbin Alumni Center (San Jacinto Blvd, Austin, TX 78712)

When: Saturday, April 14th - 8pm to 12am, no entry past 11pm (Yes, there is also an ACT on this day! Good luck!)
