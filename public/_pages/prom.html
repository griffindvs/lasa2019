Prom 2019

Where: Sheraton Austin Hotel at the Capitol (701 E 11th St, Austin, TX 78701)

When: Saturday, April 13th - 8pm to 12am, no entry past 11pm

Important Notes:

  • 1. Tickets will be sold in the lobby of the theatre. Buy early, we stop selling 15 minutes before lunch ends, and we do sometimes have to cut off the line. They may also be purchased online here.
  • 2. There is extremely limited parking, please consider using Rideshare, Uber or Lyft.
  • 3. If you are attending with someone that is not a current LBJ or LASA student they must complete and submit this form to the LASA office by April 5th. If it is not completed, they may not be able to enter the event. Please email either Lisa or Hannah if you cannot access it. There will be printed copies available where tickets are sold.

Tickets:

If you purchase online, you must pick up your tickets in person prior to the event.

  • 4/1- 4/5: $50
  • 4/8 - 4/12: $55
  • At the Door: $60

Note: Pricing for the tickets online remain the same through the weekend, however please know that there is a processing fee taken by SchoolCashOnline upon purchasing your tickets
