Congratulations

 LASA Class of 2019

    This website works to provide a variety of resources and services to the Liberal Arts and Science Academy class of 2019. Throughout the past few years, the site has provided information regarding testing, student council, and numerous events. All of these pages are listed in the Archive tab above.
    After graduating in May, the class of 2019 will be heading off to universities around the globe to continue their academic and professional careers. The website owners aim to host the site years into the future for archival usage and other alumni events. For more information regarding the LASA alumni network, head to the LASA Alumni LinkedIn Group.
